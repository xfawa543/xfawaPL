# xfawaPL 的故事 / The Story of xfawaPL

## 起源 / Origin

我是 xfawaPL 的创造者。这是一个关于界限、幻想与可能性的故事。

我是一名肢体障碍者，深受口齿不清和肌张力高等严重状况的影响。但事实就是如此，我选择面对它。

在漫长的探索中，我逐渐意识到：**残疾并非我的枷锁，而是我理解计算机的独特路径。**

我的编程启蒙源于 Minecraft 基岩版的 Addon。那些简单的 JSON 文件让我第一次窥见代码的魔力，也在我心中埋下了一颗种子：创造一门属于自己的语言。

从幻想到现实，是一段漫长的路。而 AI，成为了我跨越这段距离的桥梁。

> I am the creator of xfawaPL. This is a story about boundaries, fantasy, and possibility.
>
> I live with a physical disability, significantly affected by conditions such as slurred speech and high muscle tension. But this is the truth, and I choose to face it.
>
> Through years of exploration, I've come to realize: **My disability is not my shackles, but my unique path to understanding computers.**
>
> My programming inspiration began with Minecraft Bedrock Edition Addons. Those simple JSON files gave me my first glimpse into the magic of code, and planted a seed in me: to create a language of my own.
>
> The journey from fantasy to reality is long. And AI became the bridge that helped me cross it.

## 命名与传承 / Naming and Heritage

xfawaPL 的诞生并非偶然，它承载着一段历史的延续。

它的前身是 [xfawaML](https://github.com/xfawa543/xfawaML)，一个胶水语言项目。有趣的是，在最初版本中，它曾被称为 **xfawaPL Programming Language**。

随着新语言的构想逐渐清晰，我将"PL"之名赋予了新项目 [xfawaPL](https://github.com/xfawa543/xfawaPL)，而原项目则更名为 **xfawaML**，以更准确地反映其作为胶水语言的本质。这是一种传承，也是一种进化。

"xfawa"这个名字，来源于我曾经的 Minecraft 用户名 **xiaofeng awa543**（小峰awa）。它或许不完美，但它记录着我的起点。**名称是符号，而符号背后，是人为其赋予的意义。**

> The birth of xfawaPL was no accident. It carries a legacy.
>
> Its predecessor was [xfawaML](https://github.com/xfawa543/xfawaML), a glue language project. Interestingly, in its earliest version, it was called the **xfawaPL Programming Language**.
>
> As the vision for a new language crystallized, I passed the "PL" name to the new project [xfawaPL](https://github.com/xfawa543/xfawaPL), while the original was renamed to **xfawaML** to better reflect its nature as a glue language. This is an inheritance, and an evolution.
>
> The name "xfawa" comes from my old Minecraft username, **xiaofeng awa543**. It may not be perfect, but it marks my beginning. **A name is a symbol, and it is the person behind it who gives it meaning.**

## 开发模式 / Development Model

xfawaPL 采用了一种独特的协作模式：

*   **我担任架构师**：定义语法、设计特性、规划愿景。
*   **AI 作为实现者**：将我的设计转化为可执行的代码。
*   **我们共同探索**：**创造的真正边界，在于思想，而非工具。**

这种方式或许非常规，但它使我能够：
- 专注于语言设计的核心
- 突破物理限制进行创造
- 快速迭代与验证想法

> xfawaPL employs a unique model of collaboration:
>
> *   **I act as the architect**: Defining syntax, designing features, planning the vision.
> *   **AI serves as the implementer**: Translating my designs into executable code.
> *   **Together, we explore**: **The true boundary of creation lies in thought, not in the tools.**
>
> This approach may be unconventional, but it allows me to:
> - Focus on the core of language design
> - Create beyond physical limitations
> - Iterate and validate ideas rapidly
